I was born and raised in the walled city of Harar. This city is known to be rich in culture, history, and historical sites, including ancient mosques, markets, and unique architectural designs. Sometimes it is referred to as “The Living Museum.

This city is also known for the diversity of cultures and the beauty that comes along with living in harmony. Harar has earned its reputation as a place where people from different backgrounds come together, fostering a spirit of inclusivity and unity, giving the city another nickname: “city of love.”
